Profile2: Preferences

On your preferences page you can control what emails are sent you when various actions occur, as well as manage the Twitter integration for your status updates.

To manage your email notifications

  1. Click the radio buttons to adjust the settings on or off for each preference.

To manage the Twitter integration

  1. Click the checkbox to enable the Twitter integration.
  2. Add your Twitter username and password.

You can also disable the twitter integration by unchecking the checkbox. Your details will be removed automatically.

When you are finished, click Save settings. If you enabled the Twitter integration, your details will be verified with Twitter so you must be connected to the Internet at this point.
